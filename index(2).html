ABOUT

Chase Atlantic is an Australian alternative R&B and pop trio formed in Cairns in 2014. The band consists of brothers Mitchel Cave and Clinton Cave, alongside longtime friend Christian Anthony. They are globally recognized for blending dark pop, hip-hop, and rock, anchored by their breakthrough 2017 viral hit "Swim". Their first ever tour was held at Sydney, Australia in 2016.
