FIRST TOUR

Chase Atlantic's first-ever headlining tour was the Nostalgia Tour in 2015, which took place in Australia. Since then, the Australian-born band has performed in the city multiple times, scaling up from smaller venues like The Factory Theatre in 2019 to major arenas like Qudos Bank Arena in 2024.
